Health and Medical Assistance

Onboard Medical Assistance

Our airline provides onboard medical assistance to passengers who require medical attention during the flight. Our flight attendants are trained to handle medical emergencies and are equipped with medical kits containing essential supplies.

If you require medical assistance during the flight, please notify a flight attendant immediately. They will assess the situation and provide the necessary assistance. In case of a serious medical emergency, the flight crew will coordinate with medical professionals on the ground for further assistance.

Special Medical Needs

If you have special medical needs or require assistance due to a medical condition, please inform us at the time of booking or at least 48 hours before your scheduled departure. This will allow us to make necessary arrangements and accommodate your needs to the best of our abilities.

Common special medical needs include wheelchair assistance, oxygen requirements, dietary restrictions, and assistance animals. We strive to ensure a comfortable and safe travel experience for all our passengers.

Health and Safety Measures

Ensuring the health and safety of our passengers and crew is our top priority. We have implemented various health and safety measures in response to the COVID-19 pandemic, including enhanced cleaning and sanitization procedures, mandatory mask-wearing onboard, and physical distancing whenever possible.

We continue to monitor the situation closely and follow guidance from health authorities to adapt our policies and procedures as needed to protect the health and well-being of everyone onboard.

Travel Insurance

Consider purchasing travel insurance that includes coverage for medical emergencies and trip interruptions. Travel insurance can provide peace of mind knowing that you're financially protected in case of unexpected medical situations or travel disruptions.
