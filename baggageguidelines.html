Baggage Guidelines

Carry-On Baggage

Most airlines allow passengers to carry one piece of hand luggage and one personal item, such as a purse or laptop bag, into the cabin. However, size and weight restrictions may vary depending on the airline and class of service. It's important to check with your airline before your flight.

The following items are generally permitted in carry-on baggage:

  • Aerosol insecticides (not allowed in carry-on, but are allowed in checked bags as long as they are not labeled as hazardous material)
  • Air mattresses with built-in pump
  • Devices containing lithium metal or lithium ion batteries (should be carried in carry-on baggage)
  • Alcoholic beverages (with certain restrictions)

Checked Baggage

The allowance for checked baggage also varies by airline and ticket class. Economy passengers typically have a lower allowance compared to business or first-class passengers. Baggage fees may apply for additional or overweight bags.

The following items are generally permitted in checked baggage:

  • Small arms ammunitions (must be securely packed in fiber, wood or metal boxes or other packaging specifically designed to carry small amounts of ammunition)
  • Electronic devices (must be turned off and packed in a way that prevents unintentional activation)
  • Lithium ion batteries (with certain restrictions)

Restricted Items

There are certain items that are restricted or prohibited from being carried in both carry-on and checked baggage. These items may include sharp objects, flammable materials, and liquids exceeding the allowed limit. It's important to review the list of prohibited items provided by your airline or airport before packing.

Tips for Packing

Use TSA-approved locks for checked baggage.

Place essential items and valuables in your carry-on bag.

Securely pack any liquids or gels in a clear, resealable plastic bag.

Label your luggage with your name, address, and contact information.

Check the weight and size restrictions for both carry-on and checked baggage to avoid additional fees.
