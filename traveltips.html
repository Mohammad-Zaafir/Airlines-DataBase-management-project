Travel Tips

Google Flights

Google Flights is a useful flight search tool that has many features and functionalities to help you identify the best flight prices and options for both specific flight times and flexible itineraries.

Skyscanner

Skyscanner is the site for you if you are looking for a cheap flight to any destination. It allows you to enter your departure city and select "Explore everywhere" in the "To" field, and the world is your oyster.

Following The Flight Deal on Twitter

Following The Flight Deal on Twitter can provide real-time deal alerts without having to search for them yourself.

Independent Travel Websites

Many independent travel websites promise to find you the cheapest deals available, but some are flat-out scams. TPG scans a few websites that generally provide reliable airfare notifications and highlighted offerings.

Airline Deals

Booking tools can help ensure you're finding the most economical flight option for your next trip, but it's also important to use the right credit card to pay for the flight. Many airlines feature a deals section on their website, and many of these so-called deals can be underwhelming. However, sometimes the airlines offer incredible, short-term deals that will likely be fully booked before even reaching some deal-consolidating websites.

Credit Cards

Using branded airline cards to earn status and rewards specific to those airlines can also help you save money on your flights.
